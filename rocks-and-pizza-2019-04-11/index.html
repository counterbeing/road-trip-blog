Rocks and Pizza

April 11th, 2019 to April 12th, 2019

This was our first time seeing Miguel’s Pizza. We arrived late in the evening and found a place to set up camp, which is really not much more than a space in a parking lot, if you’re camping in a vehicle. But hey, for $3 you really can’t complain. The pizza is good, and the proximity to world class climbing is incredible.

It’s no surprise you see Miguel’s t-shirts at crags all around the world.
